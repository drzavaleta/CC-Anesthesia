# Aicardi Syndrome

## General

- Only in girls
- Chorioretinopathy
- Other eye problems

## Airway

- Microcephaly
- Possible cleft lip/palate

## Respiratory

- Scoliosis
- Abnl ribs
- Aspiration/frequent pneumonia

## Gastrointestinal

- Hiatal hernia
- Reflux risk

## Endocrine

- Precocious puberty

## Neurological

- Infantile spasms
- Agenesis of corpus callosum
- Microcephaly
- Severe mental retardation
- Hypotonia
- CNS tumors

## Musculoskeletal

- Kyphoscoliosis
- Abnl vertebrae

## Considerations

- Aspiration risk
- Respiratory difficulties
- Seizure medications may affect drug metabolism
